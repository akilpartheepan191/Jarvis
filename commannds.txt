open - takes command from the user and opens the application
close-presses ctrl+w to close a tab or a window
switch-presses alt+tab to switch a window
activate-activates a mode(read https://replit.com/@akil191/Jarvis-1#modes.txt for more info).
login-logins into camu.in
time-tells the time
date-tells the date
mute-mutes the volume
plus-increases the volume by 6
minus-decreases volume by 6
remainder-sets remainder for the specific date
alarm-sets alarm for a specific time
